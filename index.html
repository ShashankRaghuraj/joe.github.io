Learn About Me

When I was 3, I broke open a TV to see what was it like. That curiosity hasn't changed much 15 years later and I now spend time powering systems to function using my code. I plan to continue my learning through my bachelor's in Computer Science post my high school. My fascination and early experience in Artificial Intelligence and Computer vision have cemented my decision to pursue the path post-high school. In addition to my cs side, I love biking, playing chess, and playing the tenor saxophone and bass clarinet! I always look forward to ways where I can drive change and empower the people around me. I have started my podcast "Shashank vs the World", where I talk with industry experts to create a place where curious minds like me can learn more.
